How it works

  

    

      
🌈 Color wheel → hue

      
Each swatch hue steps by φ × 360° ≈ 137.5° — the "golden angle" — guaranteeing maximum visual separation with no clustering.

    

    

      
φ → lightness & chroma

      
Lightness descends and chroma shifts using golden-ratio fractions (1/φ ≈ 0.618, 1/φ² ≈ 0.382) for mathematically balanced contrast.

    

    

      
🔬 OKLCH color science

      
Unlike HSL, OKLCH is perceptually uniform — equal numeric steps feel equal to the human eye. Hue 50° and Hue 200° at the same L look equally bright.

    

    

      
✦ Proportion

      
Strip widths follow φ-weighted proportions (1 : 1/φ : 1/φ² …) so dominant, supporting, and accent roles emerge naturally.
